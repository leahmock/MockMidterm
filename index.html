New Media for Social Change

Physical Animation: Zoetrope

Isabel Faustino, Leah Mock, and Juan Santiago

During the making of our zoetropes, the first decision we made was the issue we would focus on. We chose climate change.

Based on the box and arrow model we made, we decided to target an everyday behavior that most people partake in: transportation from place to place. Using cars produces large amounts of carbon emissions, so we decided to try to convince people to use bicycles, a cleaner and greener form of transportation.
